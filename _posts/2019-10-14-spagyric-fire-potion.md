Mother of Fire Mead:
1) Peel ginger and turmeric; add peel to container.
2) Crush ginger and turmeric; strain juice into container.
3) Boil then simmer crushed ginger and turmeric and ground peppercorns for 30-60 minutes.
4) Add honey or other sweetener to infusion and dissolve completely.
5) Allow infusion to cool; add to container.
6) Allow anaerobic lacto-fermentation to begin.

Fire Mead:
1) Combine Mother of Fire Mead with large batch of unfermented Mother of Fire Mead recipe (i.e., steps 1-6).

Spirit of Fire:
1) Distill Fire Mead.

Body of Fire:
1) Calcinate solids from Fire Mead and Spirit of Fire.
2) Rinse calcinated solids.

Spagyric Elixir of Fire:
1) Combine Spirit of Fire and Body of Fire.
2) Allow mixture to age in a wooden cask.
